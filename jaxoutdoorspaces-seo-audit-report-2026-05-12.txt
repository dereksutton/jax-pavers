Mobile LCP at 8.17s is the most damaging technical issue on the site.
Given Google's mobile-first indexing and that most local-service searches
come from phones, this likely suppresses rankings and conversions.

Mobile speed remediation checklist:
  * Compress and convert hero/portfolio images to WebP or AVIF; serve
    responsive sizes via srcset.
  * Lazy-load below-the-fold imagery and embedded maps.
  * Defer or async non-critical JavaScript; remove unused plugins/scripts.
  * Enable HTTP/2 + Brotli, set long cache headers, use a CDN.
  * Inline critical CSS; eliminate render-blocking resources.
  * Audit the WordPress theme/builder (if applicable) for bloat - consider
    a lightweight theme like GeneratePress or Kadence.
